Paula Scher

Portrait

Graphic Design Queen

Paula Scher is the first female graphic designer to design graphics and illustrations professionally. She is one of the most influential graphic designers in the world!

Scher is known as the "master conjurer of the instantly familiar" meshing pop culture and fine art gorgeously. Her work has become iconic and entered into the American vernacular. Scher has been a partner of Pentagram, the worlds largest independent design company since 1991. And she is known for her eclectic typographic style that has influenced so many.
